## Customer Segments
- **Frontend & QA Engineering Teams** in mid‑size SaaS companies (30‑200 engineers) that rely on Puppeteer for end‑to‑end testing.  
- **Independent Developers & Freelancers** building web‑scraping or automation tools (≈ 10 k monthly active users on NPM).  
- **Digital Agencies & Marketing Ops** that run large‑scale data‑extraction jobs for clients (≈ 200 agencies in APAC).  
- **Enterprise R&D / DevSecOps Groups** needing secure, auditable browser automation (≈ 50 large enterprises).  

## Value Propositions
- **Managed, Scalable Puppeteer Cluster** – auto‑scale headless browsers on demand, eliminating local resource bottlenecks.  
- **Enterprise‑Grade Features** – built‑in video recording, step‑by‑step visual debugger, test analytics dashboard, and role‑based access control.  
- **Zero‑Maintenance SaaS** – automatic Chrome updates, security patches, and compliance (SOC 2, GDPR).  
- **Developer‑First Pricing** – pay‑as‑you‑go usage plus tiered subscriptions, no hidden infra fees.  
- **Integrations & SDKs** – ready‑made plugins for GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins, and popular test frameworks (Jest, Mocha).
