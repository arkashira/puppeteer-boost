```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for Puppeteer Boost

## Phase 1: Aware
**Trigger Event:** Developer encounters slow performance or limitations with standard Puppeteer automation.
**Friction Points:**
- Lack of awareness about Puppeteer Boost as a solution.
- Overwhelming number of automation tools available.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, confusion, skepticism.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Targeted ads on developer forums and social media.
- Blog posts and tutorials highlighting Puppeteer Boost's benefits.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to Puppeteer Boost's landing page.

## Phase 2: Consider
**Trigger Event:** Developer researches solutions to improve Puppeteer automation.
**Friction Points:**
- Difficulty comparing Puppeteer Boost with other tools.
- Concerns about cost and integration complexity.
**User Emotions:** Hopeful, cautious, analytical.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Detailed comparison charts and case studies.
- Free trial offers and demo videos.
**Metric:** Conversion rate from landing page to sign-up.

## Phase 3: Try
**Trigger Event:** Developer signs up for a free trial or demo of Puppeteer Boost.
**Friction Points:**
- Complex onboarding process.
- Limited documentation and support.
**User Emotions:** Excited, anxious, impatient.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Streamlined onboarding with guided tutorials.
- 24/7 customer support and live chat.
**Metric:** Time taken to complete onboarding and first successful automation.

## Phase 4: Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Developer successfully uses Puppeteer Boost for their projects.
**Friction Points:**
- Initial learning curve for advanced features.
- Integration issues with existing workflows.
**User Emotions:** Satisfied, confident, relieved.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Personalized onboarding sessions.
- Regular webinars and training sessions.
**Metric:** Percentage of trial users converting to paid subscribers.

## Phase 5: Expand
**Trigger Event:** Developer experiences significant productivity gains with Puppeteer Boost.
**Friction Points:**
- Limited awareness of additional features and use cases.
- Resistance to upgrading to higher-tier plans.
**User Emotions:** Enthusiastic, loyal, ambitious.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Upsell campaigns highlighting premium features.
- Loyalty programs and exclusive access to new features.
**Metric:** Average revenue per user (ARPU) and customer lifetime value (CLV).
```
